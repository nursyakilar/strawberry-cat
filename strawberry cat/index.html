About Strawberry Cat

A small space, on purpose.

We started Strawberry Cat because we couldn't find a café that let us stay. Most places want you in and out within the hour — we built ours the opposite way: soft chairs, low music, and enough quiet corners that you can read, study, or talk for as long as you like.

Our food follows the same idea. No long menus, no fuss — just a small batch of pastries, cakes, and drinks made well, with strawberries and cream doing most of the talking. If a cat happens to nap on the windowsill while you eat, that's just Tuesday here.

  • 🍓 Simple, seasonal bakes made daily
  • 🐾 A calm room built for staying, not rushing
  • ☕ Good coffee, gentle music, soft seating
